Footer alignment, balance & scale

Goal:

Make the footer mirror the header layout: logo flush left, Quick Links centered, Contact flush right.

Increase visual weight (type sizes/spacing) while staying elegant.

Keep it clean on mobile (stacked), and crisp on desktop (3-column layout).

Use consistent paddings with the header: px-6 sm:px-10 lg:px-12.

3) Spacing & scale notes

The footer logo now uses the same lock-up as the header (Prata / Inter all caps), at desktop-weighted sizes so it doesn’t feel timid against the strong dark background.

Quick Links are centered (column grid) for symmetry with the header’s center mass.

Contact is hard-right (just like the hamburger/contact pair in the header).

Paddings px-6 sm:px-10 lg:px-12 match the header, so left and right edges align across the site.

4) Accessibility & contrast

White and gold on #0E300E pass contrast nicely.

Link hover underline improves clarity.

Divider line uses border-white/15 for a subtle, premium finish.

✅ Acceptance criteria

Footer logo sits flush left, Quick Links centered, Contact flush right (mirrors header).

Increased visual weight: larger wordmark, clear link sizes, balanced spacing.

Mobile stacks cleanly (logo → links → contact).

Colors, fonts, and spacings align with your Brand Guide.
